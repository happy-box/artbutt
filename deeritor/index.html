    What does this do?

    The doodlebutt is essentially a way to make ART through a web interface, for easy porting to IRC.

    The art is safely saved with the doodlebutt bot,
    When you save, you will be prompted to choose a name for your creation, if the name is taken, it will automatically random a short string as a suffix.
    You will then be returned your creation in kinskode and IRC codes.
    kinskode is a shorthand for IRC codes that sucks - but it's easier for this page and doodlebutt to interpret this sort of code. Albeit for lazy reasons.
    To output your art, type doodle <art> in a channel where doodlbutt is, and doodlebutt will start to spew out the creation, alternatively you can copy the output from mIRC/IRC codes and paste yourself.

    The regexes for converting text to canvas is only working in Firefox currently, and I'm not too keen on shizzling with regex/encoding to get other browsers working too, yet.
